Doritos Cereal

Description

Delicacy made with two very simple ingredients: Doritos and Mountain Dew

Ingredients

  • A bag of doritos
  • A bottle of Mountain Dew

Steps

  1. Crash the doritos while still in the bag, using a heavy object, like a shoe, or the moon.
  2. Take a bowl and pure in the Mountain Dew until it's full. Drink the rest.
  3. Pure the broken doritos in the bowl. Be careful not to overdo it as we want a soupy consistency, not a sludge (you're not an animal).
  4. Enjoy as lunch, second breakfast or late dinner!
